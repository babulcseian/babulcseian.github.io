About Babul

A research enthusiast, pursuing a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) with a fully-funded scholarship at the School of ICT, University of Calabria (Cosenza, Italy) and University of College Dublin (UCD), Ireland. Mr. Babul has accomplished his master's degree at the School of Information Engineering, Huzhou University, Hangzhou, China. Also, he completed his B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from City University, Bangladesh. Furthermore, he had work experience as a lecturer at the Micro Institute of Technology from March 2018 to September 2019. Besides, Mr. Islam carried out a job as a data scientist at Zhejiang Seating Technology in China for more than two years. His interest is in the teaching profession, and he prefers to spend the majority of his time on research. He loves to share knowledge, so he decided to maintain a technical background. He enjoys immersing himself in novel knowledge.
