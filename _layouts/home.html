About Babul

A research enthusiast, pursuing a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) with a fully-funded scholarship at the School of ICT, University of Calabria (Cosenza, Italy) and University of College Dublin (UCD), Ireland. Mr. Babul has accomplished his master's degree at the School of Information Engineering, Huzhou University, Hangzhou, China. Also, he completed his B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from City University, Bangladesh. Furthermore, he had work experience as a lecturer at the Micro Institute of Technology from March 2018 to September 2019. Besides, Mr. Islam carried out a job as a data scientist at Zhejiang Seating Technology in China for more than two years. He is interested in both industry and academia. In addition, Babul prefers to spend most of his time on research. He loves to share knowledge, so he decided to maintain a technical background. He enjoys immersing himself in novel knowledge. In broad area of research interest is in the domain of Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning(DL), IoT Energy Efficiency, Graph Neural Network, Federated Learning, Bioinformatics, and IoT Healthcare. My Ph.D degree, supervised by Dr. Antonio Guerrieri, Raffaele Gravina, Declan Delaney and Giancarlo Fortino.

PhD Research Project: "Machine Learning Techniques for IoT-driven Cognitive Sustainable Environment".
